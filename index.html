Overview

The term Human-Machine Interface (“HMI”) describes the methods in which people interact with machines or systems, and is a subset of the broader User Interface (“UI”) field.

Progress in artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) are profoundly impacting HMI. Organizations must design interaction that are multimodal, natural and personalized one.

The goal of natural language (NL) and multimodal interface (Speech recognition, Keyboard, mouse entry etc.) is to enable ease-of-use for users in performing human-computer interactions.
